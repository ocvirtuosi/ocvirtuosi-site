VirtuoSi Chamber Orchestra


The VirtuoSi Chamber Orchestra 2024-2025 season is now in the books, capped off with the Spring Benefit concert held on June 1, 2025. This season, our talented youth ensemble performed several works from the Baroque and Classical periods, including Bach, Mozart, Vivaldi, Haydn and Schubert, along with the debut of Encanto Energico, an original composition from VirtuoSi's director Dr. Jiyoung Um.

The VirtuoSi Spring Benefit Concert raised awareness and funds for our partners at Good Neighbors, an international humanitarian and development NGO supporting underserved and vulnerable communities across 50 countries around the world. Watch the VirtuoSi 2025 Spring Concert.

If you are a young musician entering grades 7 to 12, consider joining VirtuoSi to serve communities through the joy of music. Auditions for the Fall 2025 season are now open. Contact Dr. Um for more info at (562) 213-3459.
