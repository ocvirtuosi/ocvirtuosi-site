Our Mission Performances Donate!
Virtuosi Chamber Music Orchestra hosts benefit concerts to raise awareness and support for local and national non-profit humanitarian organizations. For the current spring 2025 season, we are continuing our drive to support Good Neighbors, an international and development NGO supporting vulnerable communities across 50 countries to make the world a place without hunger and where people live in harmony.

Engaging with over 200 communities around the globe, Good Neighbors empowers people and transforms communities through social and economic development initiatives.

We invite you to learn more about Good Neighbors at goodneighbors.org

Join us at the Virtuosi Spring Benefit concert for an evening of great music and community. Admission is free and donations welcome with all proceeds benefiting Good Neighbors.
Virtuosi Spring Benefit Concert June 1, 2025, 6pm
300 Fischer Avenue, Costa Mesa, CA
